About

This visualization project focuses on analyzing research publications from R1 research institutes such as UVA and Virginia Tech. Currently, it looks at the raw number of publications at each university per field grouped by four domains: Life Sciences, Health Sciences, Physical Sciences, and Social Sciences. Interactivity is built into the visualization to allow readers to zoom in on each of these domains by clicking on the outer circle and get a closer look at each field of study. Clicking on the center of the visualization takes users back out to the original circle. The exact number of publications is visible by hovering over a specific histogram bar to reveal the field and its publications. Users can choose different institutes, and toggle axes in the top left box.
