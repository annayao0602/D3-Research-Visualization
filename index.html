Research Publication
Visualization

About

This visualization project focuses on analyzing the number of research publications from R1 research institutes such as UVA and Virginia Tech.

At a glance

The visualization is grouped into four domains:
Life Sciences,
Health Sciences,
Physical Sciences,
and Social Sciences.

Even Further

Each domain then has its own set of groups, which have separate fields (ex. Life Sciences > Neuroscience > Cognitive Neuroscience).

Interactivity

Field publications are visible on hover, users can also zoom in by clicking on each domain to see fields separated by group from just that domain.
